About

Nice Work

Nice Work Jazz Ensemble brings a modern sensibility to the jazz tradition. Originals, arrangements, and the occasional deep-cut standard — always in service of the music and the moment.

The ensemble draws from the workshop tradition of Charles Mingus: structured enough to guide the music, free enough to let it breathe.
